They Are Back –
Favorite McDonald’s Happy Meal Toys!

Just announced – 15 of the old toys from the 1980s to early 2000s will be available in limited-edition Surprise Happy Meals worldwide. Plus, two extra Disney toys will be available exclusively in the United States.
The Surprise Happy Meal boxes are available November 7 to November 11 (while supplies last!) to celebrate the 40th anniversary of McDonald’s Happy Meals.
