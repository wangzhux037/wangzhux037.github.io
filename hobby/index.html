MY HOBBY - DRAWING

Drawing and painting have been part of my life from day one. My dad's studio used to be our living room when I was young. I remember seeing him sitting in front of his easel everyday. So I naturally started doodling and drawing as soon as I knew how to hold a pencil. I've always enjoyed drawing but I never did it consistently.

When NYC entered the lockdown in March 2020, I began working from home. I had a lot more free time after work because I didn't have to commute anymore.It was then that I decided I would pickup drawing again and that I would try to draw something everyday.

I started an Instagram account (@josie_joez) to record my journey. I started posting my drawings online and because of that I've met people and I've made friends from all over the world. It's been almost a year since I started drawing consistently and it has been so rewarding!
